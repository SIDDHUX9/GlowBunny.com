Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Gentle Cleanser

Use a mild, fragrance-free foaming cleanser to avoid irritation.

Recommended: Vanicream Gentle Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Soothing Toner

Apply an alcohol-free toner with soothing ingredients like centella.

Recommended: Rice Toner

Step 3: Hydrating Serum

Use a niacinamide serum to control oil and soothe sensitive skin.

Recommended: The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

Step 4: Lightweight Moisturizer

Apply an oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer suitable for sensitive skin.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair

Step 5: Sunscreen

Use a mineral sunscreen with SPF 30+ to avoid irritation.

Recommended: EltaMD UV Clear SPF 46

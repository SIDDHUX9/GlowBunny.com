Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Gentle Cleanser

Use a mild gel or foaming cleanser to balance both oily and dry areas.

Recommended: CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Alcohol-Free Toner

Apply a toner with ingredients like witch hazel for oily areas and hyaluronic acid for dry areas.

Recommended: Thayers Witch Hazel Toner

Step 3: Hydrating Serum

Use a lightweight serum that hydrates dry areas without making oily zones greasy.

Recommended: The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

Step 4: Lightweight Moisturizer

Use an oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer that hydrates without clogging pores.

Recommended: Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel

Step 5: Sunscreen

Use a lightweight sunscreen to protect your skin from UV damage.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Anthelios Ultra-Light SPF 50

Evening Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Double Cleansing

Start with an oil-based cleanser, followed by a foaming cleanser.

Recommended: DHC Deep Cleansing Oil + CeraVe Foaming Cleanser

Step 2: Exfoliation (2-3 times a week)

Use a gentle chemical exfoliant to remove dead skin cells and unclog pores.

Recommended: Paula's Choice BHA Liquid Exfoliant

Step 3: Balancing Serum

Use a serum with niacinamide or hyaluronic acid to even out skin texture.

Recommended: The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

Step 4: Night Cream

Apply a nourishing but lightweight night cream.

Recommended: CeraVe PM Facial Moisturizing Lotion

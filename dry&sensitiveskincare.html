Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Hydrating Cleanser

Use a gentle, non-foaming hydrating cleanser to maintain moisture.

Recommended: CeraVe Hydrating Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Soothing Toner

Apply an alcohol-free toner with hydrating ingredients like rose water.

Recommended: Klairs Supple Preparation Toner

Step 3: Hydrating Serum

Use a hyaluronic acid serum to deeply hydrate dry skin.

Recommended: The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid 2% + B5

Step 4: Nourishing Moisturizer

Apply a thick, ceramide-based moisturizer to lock in hydration.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair

Step 5: Sunscreen

Use a hydrating mineral sunscreen with SPF 30+.

Recommended: EltaMD UV Restore SPF 40

Evening Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Double Cleanse

Start with micellar water if wearing makeup, then use a hydrating cleanser.

Recommended: Bioderma Sensibio H2O + CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser

Step 2: Gentle Exfoliation (2x per week)

Use a mild lactic acid exfoliant to remove dead skin cells.

Recommended: The Ordinary Lactic Acid 5% + HA

Step 3: Hydrating Serum

Apply a serum with hyaluronic acid and ceramides for extra moisture.

Recommended: COSRX Snail Mucin 96 Essence

Step 4: Night Moisturizer

Use a thick, nourishing moisturizer to repair the skin overnight.

Recommended: CeraVe Moisturizing Cream

Step 5: Soothing Treatment (if needed)

Apply a calming balm or oil for extra hydration and barrier repair.

Recommended: Laneige Cica Sleeping Mask

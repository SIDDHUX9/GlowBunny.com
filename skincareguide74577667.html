Skincare Guide

Best practices for every skin type.

Normal Skin

Balanced hydration & oil production.

  • Use a gentle cleanser.
  • Apply lightweight moisturizer.
  • Use sunscreen daily.

Oily Skin

Excess oil & shine.

  • Use a foaming cleanser.
  • Choose oil-free moisturizers.
  • Use blotting papers.

Dry Skin

Rough, flaky texture.

  • Use hydrating cleanser.
  • Apply rich moisturizer.
  • Avoid hot showers.

Combination Skin

Oily T-zone, dry cheeks.

  • Use gentle cleanser.
  • Apply different moisturizers.
  • Use targeted treatments.

Sensitive Skin

Easily irritated.

  • Choose fragrance-free products.
  • Avoid harsh exfoliants.
  • Patch test new products.

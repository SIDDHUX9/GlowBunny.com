Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Gentle Cleanser

Use a mild, pH-balanced cleanser that removes excess oil without drying.

Recommended: Vanicream Gentle Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Hydrating Toner

Apply an alcohol-free toner with soothing and hydrating ingredients.

Recommended: Klairs Supple Preparation Toner

Step 3: Balancing Serum

Use a serum with niacinamide to balance oil production and hydrate dry areas.

Recommended: The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

Step 4: Lightweight Moisturizer

Apply a gel-based moisturizer for hydration without clogging pores.

Recommended: Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel

Step 5: Sunscreen

Use a non-greasy sunscreen with SPF 30+ to protect sensitive skin.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Anthelios Mineral SPF 50

Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Hydrating Cleanser

Use a gentle, non-foaming cleanser to retain moisture.

Recommended: CeraVe Hydrating Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Alcohol-Free Hydrating Toner

Apply a toner with ingredients like rose water or glycerin.

Recommended: Klairs Supple Preparation Toner

Step 3: Hyaluronic Acid Serum

Use a hydrating serum to lock in moisture.

Recommended: The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid 2% + B5

Step 4: Rich Moisturizer

Apply a thick moisturizer to keep skin hydrated all day.

Recommended: Cetaphil Moisturizing Cream

Step 5: Sunscreen with Hydrating Ingredients

Use a sunscreen with SPF 30+ that also moisturizes.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair SPF 30

Evening Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Oil-Based Cleanser

Start with an oil cleanser to remove dirt and makeup gently.

Recommended: DHC Deep Cleansing Oil

Step 2: Hydrating Cleanser

Follow up with a non-foaming, gentle cleanser.

Recommended: CeraVe Hydrating Facial Cleanser

Step 3: Hydrating Serum

Apply a serum with ceramides and peptides.

Recommended: Drunk Elephant B-Hydra Intensive Hydration Serum

Step 4: Night Cream

Use a thick night cream to nourish skin overnight.

Recommended: First Aid Beauty Ultra Repair Cream

Step 5: Facial Oil (Optional)

Seal in moisture with a lightweight facial oil.

Recommended: The Ordinary 100% Plant-Derived Squalane

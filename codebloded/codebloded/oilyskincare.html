Morning Routine - Step by Step

Step 1: Cleanser

Use a gentle foaming cleanser with salicylic acid to remove excess oil and dirt.

Recommended: CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser

Step 2: Toner

Apply an alcohol-free toner with witch hazel to balance oil production.

Recommended: Thayers Witch Hazel Toner

Step 3: Serum

Use a lightweight, oil-free Vitamin C serum to brighten the skin.

Recommended: TruSkin Vitamin C Serum

Step 4: Moisturizer

Apply an oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer to keep skin hydrated.

Recommended: Neutrogena Hydro Boost Gel

Step 5: Sunscreen

Use SPF 30+ with a matte finish to protect against sun damage.

Recommended: La Roche-Posay Anthelios SPF 50
